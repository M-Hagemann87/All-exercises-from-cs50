Dogs

"Dogs, the best man's friend"

The dog is a domesticated descendant of the gray wolf. Also called the domestic dog, it was selectively bred from a population of wolves during the Late Pleistocene by hunter-gatherers. The dog was the first species to be domesticated by humans, over 14,000 years ago and before the development of agriculture. (Wikipedia)
- Lifespan: 10 – 13 years (Medium size)
- Daily sleep: 12 – 14 hours (Adult)
- Gestation period: 58 – 68 days
- Class: Mammalia
- Family: Canidae
- Genus: Canis
